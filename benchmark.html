Benchmark

Following the existing vision-language pre-training models, we employ a dual-encoder architecture for vision-language representation learning. Since the encoders of visual and textual modalities are decoupled, we explore different encoder architectures. Benchmark code is available at Mindspore and Pytorchproject.
